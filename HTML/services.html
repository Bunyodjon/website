Visualization of State of the Union Addresses

This is a Shiny app that visualizes State of the Union(SOTU) addresses of last three president of America. The first tab of the app allows users to see differences between presedential speeches using comparison clouds. The second tab allows users to see association of SOTU speeches with the NRC lexicon which is ten basic emotions (anger, fear, anticipation, trust, surprise, sadness, joy, disgust, negative and positive). Third tab allows users to view sentiment score of speeches from the beginning till the end of a speech, and compre how sentiment score differs between speeches and from sentence to sentence.
